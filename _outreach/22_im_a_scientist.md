I participated in the outreach program “I’m a Scientist – Get Me Out of Here!”, a nationwide dialogue initiative connecting school students directly with researchers. During the robotics-themed week, I answered students’ questions about what robots are, how robotics is applied today, and what opportunities and risks the technology brings for society. Through written live chats and open Q&A sessions, I discussed both my current research and my professional journey, offering insights into everyday scientific work while engaging in direct exchange with curious and thoughtful students.
