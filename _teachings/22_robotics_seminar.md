I served as an advisor in the Robotics Seminar, mentoring 3 students per year. My responsibilities included:

**Student supervision**
- Advising on presentation preparation and delivery
- Supporting the development and refinement of written reports or papers
- Providing guidance on technical content and research methodology

**Seminar participation**
- Actively contributing to seminar discussions alongside students, PhD students, and the supervising professor (Oliver Brock)
- Offering feedback on ongoing work and encouraging critical analysis

**Focus topics:** Robotics, perception, computational models of intelligence
