I led a three-week exercise module introducing foundational robot control concepts through guided instruction and hands-on experimentation.

Responsibilities included:

- Delivering structured instructional sessions on robot control fundamentals
- Supervising and advising student teams
- Reviewing implementations and providing feedback
- Evaluating and grading student submissions
- Moderating live robot demonstrations and oral questioning
- Contributing to examination design

**Focus topics:** Visual servoing, perception-action coupling, real-time control
